how to make a milkshake :3

Description

milkshakes are a delicacy especially in the summer time as they are made with icecream to cool you off the heat

Ingredients

  • milk
  • icecream of your choice
  • whipped cream

steps

  1. you put your icecream and milk into a blender then blend
  2. pour your milkshake into a cup
  3. top it off with whipped cream and topping of your choice
